Custom Tags are entirely optional labels that can be attached to one or more bets. They provide another layer for filtering, and thus allow for more specific analysis.

You can create, edit and delete your tags here, and then assign them to, or remove them from, bets in the Bet History table by clicking on each bet's 'Manage Tags' button.

Some advice: While it's a good practice to consistently label your bets, running analyses through multiple filters on a sample that isn't sufficiently large can lead to misleading statistics. For example, if you have a total of 200 NBA spread bets, the resulting analytics should be informative and useful. However, if you filter the data only for games where the Indiana Pacers were home dogs, the sample size may become too small to provide reliable insights.
